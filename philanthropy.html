Project ScArE
Benefitting the UCSF Neuro-Oncology Fund

Every Halloween, our members pair up with the lovely ladies of Alpha Phi to host a three night haunted house for charity. This past year, the group rallied the funds for the UCSF neuro-oncology fund due to the fact that the mother of one of our members was treated there for the entire course of her illness. In the end, we raised nearly $3,200, more than doubling our totals in previous years. It was also...like...super scary.

Paddy Murphy Competing for a cause

Come spring time, we organize a week long competition among the sororities of Northwestern's Panhellenic Association. The events include a carnival, several athletic competitions, and even a variety show. Proceeds this past year went to Go 4 The Goal, an organization with the mission of improving the lives of children battling cancer.

Park School Service partnership

The Staff of Park School believe in the commitment to educate all students regardless of their disability or socio-economic background. Attempting to help further their goal, we assembled several trips to the Park School this spring to assist in the daily activities and have fun with the students there.

Dance Marathon

One of the more exciting events of the year, not only for our brothers but for the entire Northwestern Campus at large, Dance Marathon has been a rallying point for our chapter and our efforts are only growing. Raising over $72,000 in the past year, finally qualifying for the Large Team Category, and now having two brothers on the exec committee, we're excited to support the Starlight Foundation this year.
